Vinny Privacy Policy

Last updated: 07 Jul, 2024

Effective 07 Jul, 2024

Introduction

This privacy policy outlines how we handle your data when you use the Vinny bot on Discord.


Guild Configuration

We store guild configurations in our database. Upon bot removal, all configuration data will be deleted.


Moderation Data

Moderation data is stored permanently in our database. After guild removal, moderation data will be hidden from the web interface.


User Authentication

User authentication data is collected during Discord login and retained only for the duration of the OAuth2 session.


Data Security

We do not share your data with any third parties. All data is securely stored within our servers.



For GDPR inquiries, send an e-mail to gdpr@vinny.polarite.club (gdpr at vinny dot polarite dot club)
